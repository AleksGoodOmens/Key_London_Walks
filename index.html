The life and times of King`s Cross
Join us on an historical tour of King`s Cross! From gasworks to present day Google, The tour will explore the rich and remarkable past that makes up this thriving London hub. From railways and radicals to an industrial past of brassworks and bone crushing. Hear about livings made from quail and grain and lives lived by coal and canal, the ice trade and the notorious vice trade! A place of dance raves among rundown Victorian remnants rubbing shoulders with stunning Georgian Architecture….. all within the heart of King`s Cross.
Duration 90 min
Distance 1 km
